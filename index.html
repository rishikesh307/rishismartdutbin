Our Mission

1. Develop an automated smart dustbin powered by an ESP32 controller to efficiently sort and manage waste.
2. Implement sensors and actuators to detect and segregate different types of waste, such as recyclables, organic materials, and non-recyclables.
3. Utilize IoT connectivity to enable real-time monitoring and remote management of the smart dustbin, enhancing efficiency and convenience.
4. Incorporate machine learning algorithms to continuously improve waste sorting accuracy and adapt to changing waste compositions.
5. Collaborate with local communities, municipalities, and businesses to deploy smart dustbins in public spaces, residential areas, and commercial establishments.
6. Educate and raise awareness about waste management practices, emphasizing the importance of recycling and responsible disposal.
7. Continuously innovate and iterate on the smart dustbin technology to stay at the forefront of waste management solutions and contribute to a cleaner, greener future.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a cleaner and more sustainable environment by revolutionizing waste management through the integration of cutting-edge technology.
